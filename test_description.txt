Test 1: Sign Up
1. Open the DemoBlaze website.
2. Click "Sign up".
3. Enter a new username and password.
4. Click "Sign up" button.
5. Verify the alert says "Sign up successful."

Test 2: Log In
1. Click "Log in".
2. Enter valid credentials.
3. Click "Log in" button.
4. Verify that the username is displayed on the top bar.

Test 3: Log Out
1. Click "Log out" button.
2. Verify that the "Log in" button is visible again.

Test 4: Buy Product
1. Click "Laptops" category.
2. Choose a laptop (e.g., Sony vaio i5).
3. Click "Add to cart".
4. Accept the alert.
5. Go to "Cart".
6. Click "Place Order".
7. Fill the form with random data.
8. Click "Purchase".
9. Verify "Thank you for your purchase" text is displayed.
